Demigod

You will have to fight one of four desert demigods, depending on which canopic jar you have: Apmeken, Crondis, Het or Scabaras. They can be dispatched easily with recoil and poison. However, they will despawn rather quickly, so ironmen or skillers may want to use a Deathtouched Dart instead.

Priest

The final enemy is a possessed priest. He can be defeated with the same methods as the demigods. However, he has much less LP, so a Deathtouched Dart shouldn't be necessary.
