Entranan firebird

For this part of the quest, you will need ice gloves, which are dropped by the Ice Queen. If you do not own this item yet, you will have have to defeat her. Recoil and poison work fine.

The Entranan firebird is somewhat more difficult, due to the restrictions on Entrana. You can bring runes and recoil rings normally, which allows you to tag and recoil the bird. Alternatively, you can bring the ingredients for a few poison bombs. For safety reasons, you may want to bring a bowstring and logs, and craft a bow while on Entrana. Wielding this bow without ammo will prevent you from accidentally attacking the firebird normally.
