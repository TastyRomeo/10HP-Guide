Cerberus

As a favour to Drezel, you must defeat the guard dog Cerberus. You can recoil it easily, but a defensive ability (Reflect, Provoke, Revenge) must be used in order for the kill to count. Alternatively, since he awards scaled Combat XP, you could recoil him to 1 LP and then finish him off.

Monk of Zamorak

You must kill one of the monks to get a Golden Key drop. Again, recoiling with a defensive ability works just fine. Ironmen will have to 51% for the drop. Note that the account must not have progressed too far in the Myreque quest line to be able to see (and attack) the monks.
